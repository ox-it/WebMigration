Student Systems Support Centre

Student Systems training courses

All training courses will take place at the IT Services Office, 23-38 Hythe Bridge Street, Oxford, OX1 2EP. Please go to the location page for information on how to book onto training and further details about where to find us. Please also familiarise yourself with the booking conditions.

The following courses are available:

  • OSS Navigation and Data Views
  • College Graduate Admissions
  • UAB Graduate Admissions
  • Student Registration: Colleges and Departments
  • Central Graduate Admissions
  • VRO Admissions
  • Student Administration: On-Course Student Record Keeping
  • Student Administration: On-Course Research Student Record Keeping
  • OxCORT: Tutorial Office Administration

OSS Navigation and Data Views

Course details 
Pre-requsities None
Duration ½ day
Audience Staff who need to access the data held on OSS.
Note: Anyone can attend this course although preference will be given to those who are required to attend as a prerequisite for the UAB and College Graduate Admissions courses.
Course Content
  • Overview of Oxford Student Systems
  • Log into OSS
  • Navigate around OSS
  • Carry out basic searches
  • Change Responsibility
  • Log into the Data View interface
  • Search for data
  • Download the data to a .csv file and save as an Excel spreadsheet

OSS Navigation QRG (452kb)
OSS Data View Introduction QRG (821kb)
Course dates
Tuesday 16 September, 2014, 09.30-12.30
Wednesday 29 October, 2014, 09.30-12.30

College Graduate Admissions

Course details 
Pre-requisites OSS Navigation and Data Views
 Duration 1 day
 Audience College Administrators who are responsible for updating graduate applications.
Course content
  • Overview of the graduate admissions process
  • Manage applications within the application deadline cycle
  • Print out applications and supporting materials using the Data Views
  • Enter decisions and conditions into OSS
  • Enter funding information
  • Manage Visa applications
  • Manage documentation sent in to fulfil conditions
  • Student Registration (additonal section at the end of the course)

*Note: In addition to the above, the Student Registration Interface training will also be covered at the end of the training course, this part of the course is optional and will take 30 minutes, for those who would like to stay for this the finish time will be 15.30.

Student Registration: Colleges and Departments

Course Details 
Pre-requisites None - although familirisation with Dataviews would be an advantage.
Duration ½ day

  • Overview of the registration process
  • Log into the Registration Interface

  • Matriculation and SLC Interface

OxCORT: Tutorial Office Administration

Course Details  
Pre-requsites None 
Duration 2 days
Audience Tutorial Office Staff
Course Content

Day one: Tutorial Office Administration

  • Overview of OxCORT
  • OxCORT Settings
  • Giving others access to OxCORT
  • Matching students to OPT
  • Entering weighting and Stint Data
  • Searching for Reports
  • Logging in as other roles

Day two: Tutorial Office Payments

  • Recap from Day one
  • View or process tutorial reports
  • Validate tutor teaching
  • Calculate payments
  • Manage tutor payment data
  • List payments due from other colleges
  • Reporting
  • View tutorial office hours by organising tutor statistical reporting
Manuals Please see the documentation page on the OxCORT website.
Course Dates Day one: Tuesday 23 September 2014, 09.30-16.00
Day two: Thursday 25 September 2014, 09.30-16.00
