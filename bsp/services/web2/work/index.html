Work Requests from the service

How to request a new site

To request the creation of a new website please complete the New site set-up form (32kb). New site set-up guidance notes (53kb) are available.

Completed forms should be sent to webcms@admin.ox.ac.uk. 

You can also discuss any initial ideas you may have by contacting x80088.

How to request a change or development

To request development of a new template, web application, web form or other new feature for your existing site or change to any existing feature/template please complete the Web Development Request Form (75kb)

Completed forms should be sent to webcms@admin.ox.ac.uk

You will then be contacted with regard to finalising your request and time scales.

Current work slate

The team’s current work commitments and future requests are maintained in a spreadsheet which is reviewed periodically by the Web Service Steering Group which prioritises the work to be undertaken.
