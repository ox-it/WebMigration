Service Description

The Projects Training Team design and deliver training courses and produce support materials in order to maximise the effective use of new and upgraded IT systems at the University of Oxford.

The team appreciate that the introduction of a new or upgraded IT system is not just a technological implementation, but involves changes to business processes and the way people perform their day-to-day jobs.  The training team work closely with the project team and the sponsoring department to identify training requirements, business processes and real-world scenarios that can be used during training.  This enables them to produce training programmes and courses that are specifically tailored for the University of Oxford, enhances delegates’ understanding of IT systems, and enables them to improve their performance thereby maximising the business investment in new and upgraded IT systems. 

Training courses are designed and delivered to meet the TAP Learning Programme standards set by the Training Foundation, and the team has become a Gold Partner of this organisation. The TAP methodology ensures a structured approach to planning and delivery of training. This is based on a delivery style that encourages active participation from delegates and focuses on skills transfer techniques, rather than a more traditional lecture style of training.

Core Services offered

The Training Team can work with you to:

  • Advise on the most appropriate training strategy for your IT system implementation, and create a training strategy document.
  • Perform a training needs analysis.
  • Create trainer-led courses, including comprehensive training scripts, presentations, training data, delegate exercises and valid tests to the TAP standard.
  • Deliver trainer-led training courses to the TAP standard.
  • Create support materials, such as user manuals, quick reference guides and online simulations.
  • Advise on the set up of training databases, including loading of training data, overnight refresh of data, and functionality updates.
  • Advise you on Train-the-Trainer courses, and assist with a gradual transition of courses and materials to your departmental trainers.
  • Provide training administration services including room and catering bookings, management of course schedule and training invitations, creation and maintenance of project training website, and provision of course evaluation and attendance statistics.
  • Provide managed IT training rooms with either 6 or 8 delegate PCs, one trainer PC, interactive whiteboard and projector.  The rooms can cater for delegates with disabilities including a height adjustable desk, chairs with arms and a portable hearing aid loop.  Hot and cold drinks are available, and catering can be arranged (although there will be a charge for this). 

Additional Services offered

Other services the team can provide include:

  • Delivery of online webinars, systems demonstrations and presentations.
  • Floorwalking support in departments during initial go-live.
  • Facilitation of events, such as meetings, workshops, briefings or focus groups.
  • Coaching sessions for staff who require more support using business systems.
  • One-to-one sessions for users with particular needs, who are unable to attend classroom training courses.
  • Training testers who will take part in User Acceptance Testing.
  • Participation in User Acceptance Testing.

Evaluation

All training courses are assessed by the delegates using an online course evaluation in which they rate statements about the course, the trainer, the training administration and the training room choosing either “strongly agree”, “agree”, “disagree” or “strongly disagree”.  The team aim to have 95% of people agree or strongly agree with the statements for non-project IT training, and 90% of people agree or strongly agree with the statements for project implementation training.

All feedback is reviewed, evaluated and used to improve the course materials and delivery for future courses.

Charging

The team are currently charged out at the standard Programme & Projects Delivery daily rate, when working on system implementation projects.  Course delegates are not charged for their attendance at training courses, nor is there a charge for support materials that are available online or which are handed out during training courses.
