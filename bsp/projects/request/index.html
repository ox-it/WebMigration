Request a project

To submit a formal request for a new project

If you are considering making a formal request for a potential new project, please download and read the Project Request form. This will give you an idea of the information that you will need to provide, in order to submit a formal request. After you have submitted the form, the PMO will refer it for formal consideration by BSP and give you an indication of when you can expect to hear the outcome.

Please contact the Programme Management Office (PMO) for assistance in filling it in:

  • Tel.: 01865 (2) 84868
  • E-mail: bsppmo@admin.ox.ac.uk

To discuss informally a speculative idea

If you would like to discuss more informally an idea for a possible new project, please contact the Programme Management Office Manager:

  • Tel.: 01865 (2) 84868
  • E-mail: philippa.days@admin.ox.ac.uk

PMO will direct your request to the appropriate team within BSP, and will send you email notification of which team will be handling your enquiry and that team’s contact details. You should then pick up your dialogue with that team directly.
