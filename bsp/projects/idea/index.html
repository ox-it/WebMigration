Contact us about an idea

If you would like to discuss an idea for a new project, in the first instance please contact the Project Management Office (PMO):

  • Tel.: 01865 (2) 84868
  • E-mail: bsppmo@admin.ox.ac.uk

PMO will direct your request to the appropriate team within BSP, and will send you email notification of which team will be handling your enquiry and that team’s contact details. You should then pick up your dialogue with that team directly.

In certain cases, e.g. if your idea is an extension of an existing project that BSP has undertaken for you, you may already have a relationship with the relevant team – in which case you are welcome to approach that team directly.
