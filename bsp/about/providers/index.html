UAS IT providers

IT services are currently provided to University Administration and Services (UAS) by three providers: BSP, ICT ST (ICT Support Team) and OUCS (Oxford University Computing Services). We rely on each others' specialist areas and work closely together to provide the overall service to UAS.

The University has embarked on a programme to bring together these three ICT providers in UAS. More information on the ICT Central Coordination Programme, which is sponsored by the Registrar, can be found on the ICCP website.

Service provider
 What it provides to UAS
BSP
  • Development and support of core administrative systems, e.g.:
    • Financials
    • Student Systems
    • HR
    • Research Services
    • Groupware
    • DARS
  • Project management
  • Application development (creation/modification)
  • Web services
  • IT infrastructure support
  • Training on business systems
ICTST
  • Desktop support and security
  • Sserver infrastructure support
  • File and print services
  • Application packaging and delivery
  • Computer configuration and deployment
  • Hardware, peripherals and software purchasing
  • UAS Network provision, support and security
  • Backup/recovery of user and shared data
OUCS
  • Internet connection and backbone network
  • Online security, spam filtering and firewall
  • Oxford Single Sign-On
  • User data to BSP and ICT
  • Telecoms and wireless
  • Central NExus e-mail and calendaring provision
  • WebLearn
  • Backups and long-term archiving
  • Software provision
  • Training on software packages, programming technologies and teaching and learning resources e.g. WebLearn
  • Help centre
  • Research support
  • Mobile Oxford
